# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want CreativeStack to be a welcoming space for everyone who works in and around the creative industry — designers, strategists, writers, producers, engineers, and everyone in between. Regardless of experience level, background, or the tools you use day to day, you should feel comfortable contributing.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Assume good intent, and extend patience when something is unclear
- Focus on the idea, not the person
- Give credit where it is due
- Accept feedback gracefully, and offer it kindly

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or dismissive language
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Publishing someone's private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel associated with CreativeStack.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code, please contact the maintainer at camawjones@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and kept confidential.

The maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project.
